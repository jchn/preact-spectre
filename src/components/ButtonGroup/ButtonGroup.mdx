### Block

Add the `block` prop to make the ButtonGroup take up the entire width of its parent:

<Playground>
  <ButtonGroup block>
    <Button>first button</Button>
    <Button>second button</Button>
    <Button>third button</Button>
  </ButtonGroup>
</Playground>
