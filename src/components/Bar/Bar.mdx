### Small

Add the `sm` prop to show a small version of the Bar component:

<Playground>
  <Bar sm>
    <Bar.Item
      role="progressbar"
      progress={50}
      background="#818bd5"
      aria-valuenow="50"
      aria-valuemin="0"
      aria-valuemax="100"
    />
    <Bar.Item
      role="progressbar"
      progress={25}
      aria-valuenow="25"
      aria-valuemin="0"
      aria-valuemax="100"
    />
  </Bar>
</Playground>

### Slider

Add the `slider` prop and `Bar.SliderButton` to turn it into a slider:

<Playground>
  <Bar slider>
    <Bar.Item
      role="progressbar"
      progress={25}
      aria-valuenow="25"
      aria-valuemin="0"
      aria-valuemax="100"
    >
      <Bar.SliderButton />
    </Bar.Item>
  </Bar>
</Playground>
