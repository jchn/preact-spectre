### Responsive

To change the way the video resizes set the `responsive` prop to one of `4-3`, `1-1` or `true`:

<Playground>
  <Video responsive="4-3">
    <iframe
      width="560"
      height="315"
      src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/eRsGyueVLvQ"
      frameborder="0"
      allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture"
      allowfullscreen
    />
  </Video>
</Playground>
